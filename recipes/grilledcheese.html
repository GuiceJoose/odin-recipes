Grilled Cheese

Description

This is how to make a grilled cheese, which I'm not sure why you would need a recipe for.

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of bread
  • 2 sheets of yellow dairy-based polymer
  • Lots of butter

Steps

  1. Melt a reasonable amount of butter in a pan over medium heat
  2. Place bread slice in pan
  3. Place yellow dairy-based polymer onto bread
  4. Cover with other bread slice
  5. Cook on medium heat until nice and brown
  6. Flip and cook other side until brown
  7. Put on plate and cut diagonally
