Fast Pizza Dough

Description

This is a fast pizza dough recipe for when your dumb lazy ass forgot to make dough the night before. It takes ~30 minutes to make.

Ingredients

  • 3/4 cup bread flour
  • 3/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 3/4 tsp yeast
  • 1/2 tsp sugar
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 3/4 cup 120-130 degree F water

Steps

  1. Mix all dry ingredients and oil
  2. Add water and mix, use more flour if dough is too wet
  3. Cover and let sit for 20-30 minutes
  4. Spread out in baking sheet and assemble pizza
